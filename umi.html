Creative Direction - UMI
In the year since I started directing UMI’s videos, I’ve watched as she’s gone from recording songs in her dorm to touring across the country, racking up millions of views and streams along the way.
High School

I served as co-director on High School, UMI’s latest release, along with Eddie Mandell. We worked with UMI to take the video from conceptualization to execution with a small budget of $5000. Along with our producer Nick Scully, we directed and managed 50 extras and a crew of over 10 people over a two day shoot. I also served as director of photography, operating the camera for the duration of the shoot. In post I edited the majority of the video, as well as handled the color-grade. Additional production design, sound design and marketing was provided by our small team at ourros.

Friendzone

Friendzone, our first collaboration, was the first large-scale project that I had worked on. Along with a small team of USC students, I conceptualized and co-directed this music video with absolutely no budget, no permits, and no idea what I was doing. I handled editing and color grading along with Eddie Mandell and Aamir Khuller, as well as animated parts of the video.
